Guidelines

  1. The potential investors for projects are our Alumini and Institute itself.
  2. You are allowed to draw an advance of Rs. 10,000 at any given time. No further advance is allowed, until this is settled.
  3. Project duration: June to December. No fund will be provided after this period.
  4. Project progress review meeting will be conducted by the end of October 2020, during which the project progress report is to be submitted along with a presentation.
  5. At the end of the projectfinal project report is required to be submitted along with project demos and presentation.
  6. In case, the Project Lead is unable to continue with the project, he/she will have to identify a Project Lead from the team members with the approval of Dean Students.
